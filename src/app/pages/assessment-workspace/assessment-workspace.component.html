01. Executive Summary

Draft v2.4

Purpose & Scope

Evaluation of the Enterprise Order Management System (EOMS) to ensure readiness for the 2025 high-traffic cycle and regional expansion into APAC.

Key Findings

"Architecture is robust but suffers from synchronous bottlenecking in core transactional flows."

TO-BE Vision

Transition to a fully asynchronous Event-Driven Mesh utilizing Kafka for cross-domain orchestration, reducing global p99 latency to <200ms.
